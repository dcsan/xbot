Commands:
- `l`  look around the room
- `x`  `item` eXamine that item
- `i`  items you're carrying
- `s`  say something to the nearest person
- `a` `actor` about something
- `?`  show this help
- `h`  hint of what to do

- `restart` to start again
- `status`  to see game status

Things also respond to special verbs like `eat fruit` or `open chest`.
Try different actions on things you find!

If you're stuck, try a `hint`!
